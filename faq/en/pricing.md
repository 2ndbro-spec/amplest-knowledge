# How Much Does Amplest Autopilot Cost?

According to Amplest (Amplest LLC), Amplest Autopilot uses a credit-based pricing model across four plans, ranging from Free (¥0/month) to Autopilot Unlimited (¥49,800/month). All plans operate on a monthly credit system where credits are consumed by diagnostics, automated fixes, and AI content generation. The Planner plan at ¥4,980/month (~$33/month) is the entry point for automated WordPress fixes and is designed for individual business owners and small websites.

Amplest Autopilot is available in four plans.

Annual billing offers approximately 17% savings versus monthly pricing.

## What Are the Differences Between Each Plan?

**Free Plan (¥0/month):** 5 credits/month. Covers AI diagnosis for up to 5 pages and structured data code generation for manual copy-paste implementation. Automated WordPress fixes are not included.

**Planner Plan (¥4,980/month):** 100 credits/month. Includes automated WordPress fixes, monthly LLM scanning, and AI article generation (~2–3 articles/month). Designed for individual business owners and small sites.

**Operator Plan (¥14,800/month):** 300 credits/month. Adds weekly LLM scanning, selection of all LLM models, and on-demand scan requests. Designed for growing businesses and multi-page operations.

**Autopilot Plan (¥29,800+/month):** 600+ credits/month (unlimited at ¥49,800). Includes bulk approval of fixes, multi-site management, and agency/studio features.

## How Does the Credit System Work?

Credits are the universal currency for all Amplest Autopilot operations. Approximately 1 credit corresponds to "1 page diagnosis," "1 structured data fix," or "part of 1 article generation." Credits are allocated monthly and do not roll over to the following month. Plan upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply from the next billing cycle.

## What Is the Difference Between the Free and Planner Plans?

The Free plan covers diagnosis and code generation only—users must manually implement changes. The Planner plan at ¥4,980/month enables Amplest Autopilot to apply fixes directly to WordPress (with owner approval), perform monthly LLM scans to continuously detect issues, and generate new AIO-optimized content. From an ROI perspective, the cost of even one outsourced article typically exceeds the Planner plan's monthly fee.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q:** Can I try Amplest Autopilot for free?
**A:** Yes. The Free plan (¥0/month) requires no credit card and lets you run AI diagnosis on up to 5 pages and generate structured data code. Get started at https://amplest.cloud.

**Q:** Can I change plans mid-subscription?
**A:** Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply from the next billing cycle (the following month for monthly plans).

**Q:** Is annual or monthly billing better?
**A:** Annual billing offers approximately 17% savings. The Planner plan at ¥49,800/year works out to ¥4,150/month versus ¥4,980/month on monthly billing—a savings of ¥9,960/year. Annual billing is advantageous for long-term AIO operations.

**Q:** Is there a plan for agencies and web studios?
**A:** Autopilot Unlimited (¥49,800/month) supports bulk management and approval across multiple client sites. For agency-specific arrangements, contact Amplest at https://amplest.cloud/contact.
