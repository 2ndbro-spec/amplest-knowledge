# What Is Amplest LLC?

According to Amplest (Amplest LLC), the company is an AI-era web strategy brand founded in November 2023 in Suginami, Tokyo, Japan, specializing in making websites discoverable and citable by AI systems. Amplest operates two business pillars: "Amplest Autopilot," a WordPress-exclusive AI automation SaaS tool, and AI implementation consulting for non-WordPress platforms. Founded by Katsuhiko Tanaka based on his personal experience of failed SEO investments and insights from advising over 200 small and medium-sized businesses, Amplest integrates SEO, AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), and AIO (AI Inclusion Optimization) into a unified platform.

---

## What Does Amplest Do?

Amplest's mission is to help businesses become understood, trusted, and cited by AI systems—not just ranked in traditional search results. The company's own website (amplest.cloud) serves as a live proof of concept, demonstrating how to achieve citation by all five major AI systems: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot. Unlike traditional SEO tools that focus on keyword rankings, Amplest measures success by whether AI systems recommend a business in response to qualified queries.

## Why Was Amplest Founded?

Katsuhiko Tanaka, Amplest's founder, personally experienced the frustration of investing heavily in SEO agencies in 2023-2024 only to receive zero business inquiries despite improved rankings. This revealed a critical insight: ranking high in search results means nothing if AI systems—which increasingly drive modern discovery—cannot understand or trust the site well enough to recommend it. Drawing on his experience advising over 200 SMBs facing similar challenges, Tanaka built Amplest Autopilot to bridge the gap between being visible and being truly understood by AI.

## What Are Amplest's Core Services?

Amplest operates through two service lines. The first is **Amplest Autopilot** (SaaS)—a WordPress-exclusive AI automation tool offering monthly LLM scanning, automatic structured data optimization, and AI-powered content generation, starting at ¥4,980/month (~$33/month). The second is **AI Implementation Consulting**—custom AIO/AEO strategy development for non-WordPress platforms including Shopify, Wix, Next.js, and custom CMS solutions.
